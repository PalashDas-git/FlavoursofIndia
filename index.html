Welcome to Our Home

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Kolkata, Flavours of India brings you an unforgettable culinary journey through the diverse regions of India. Our master chefs craft each dish with passion, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're seeking an intimate dinner for two, a celebratory feast with family, or a grand banquet for your special occasion, our elegant spaces and warm hospitality create the perfect setting for every moment.
